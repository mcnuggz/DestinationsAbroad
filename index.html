Our Mission

We provide higher education students with comprehensive travel packages for their studies and mission work abroad. We offer cultural awareness training that will help students to acclimate to destination's language and culture. We assist students in obtaining the necessary passports and visas. We offer risk reducing services to avoid unwanted experiences abroad.

Our Programs

Our programs are led by a Destinations Abroad specialist. The specialists are in destination experts and are responsible for the health, safety and educational growth of all participants. Specialists will follow a pre-determined schedule of after school and weekend activities throughout the destination. The independent programs we offer provide students with the opportunity to complete their studies and mission work while giving them the flexiblity to discover the destination on their own.

Short-term study abroad or mission work (dependent or independent): Our short term programs are defined as one to four week trips. They are offered in a variety of destinations around the world and can be molded to fit any area of study.

Semester long abroad or mission work (dependent or independent): Similar to the short-term study abroad or mission work programs, this program is offered in a variety of destinations around the world and can be molded to fit any area of study. Semester long programs are extended to provide participants with a full semseter of exploration.

Year-long study abroad or mission work (dependent): The lengthiest program we offer is our year-long arrangements. While these programs are offered across the world participants will be placed using a third-party partner. Programs offered year-round are centered on specific areas of study and can be very fulfilling. Unlike our short-term and semester long programs, the year-long curriculum is provided as dependent study abroad option only.
