What do we do?

Be culture savvy before departure

Enroll in our cultural awareness classes to ensure you are up to speed on the Do's and Don'ts in your new home away from home. Our one night a week classes provide students with a crash course on language and culture to ensure you feel completely prepared to take on the world-- literally!

Passport-check! Visa-check!

Traveling abroad can be stressful and a bit overwhelming. Destinations Abroad can help you obtain all of the needed documentation including passports and visas. Some destinations may require you to obtain separate documents while in destination. We can help with that too.

Apply for health insurance

Destinations Abroad requires that all participants obtain global health insurance. Participants may choose to obtain health insurance through their current provider or they can visit Chubb's website to learn more.

Currency Exchange

Before traveling abroad, investigate the currency of the country you are traveling to. You will likely have a lot of questions like: How much is my dollar worth? How much should I take with me? Can I convert some of it there? What if I run out of money? Your study abroad advisor can help answer all of these questions prior to departure, just make sure to write a list with all of your questions.

Orientation

Prior to traveling abroad your Desintaions Abroad advisor will schedule a four-hour orientation. Orientations are typically held two to three weeks prior to departure. All participants must attend or they will not be allowed to travel. The orientation is structured around three major objectives: allow students to network with other individuals who will be in destination, review required documents and ensure the student is fully prepared for the voyage that awaits, and lastly for any last minute questions or concered to be answered.

Packing for your trip

There are many things to take into account when packing for your adventure abroad. Consider things like the weather, government, dress codes, culturally accepted clothing and clothing that you might want to purchase while there. Don't forget to take items that may not be readily available overseas like your perscription drugs and personal hygiene necessities. Pack light and remember to check the airlines luggage restrictions. Some airlines allow between one and two check bags plus a carry-on.

24 Hour in-destination support

From the moment you arrive, our dedicated team of in-destination support professionals is at your service to make sure you are taken care of. Whether it is providing last minute transportation, giving direction on class credits or just answering a question Destinations Abroad is available 24 hours a day to make sure your travels abroad goes smoothly.
