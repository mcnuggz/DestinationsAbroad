Program Options

Short-term programs in Australia

Destinations Abroad can surely find a program that fits your higher education needs. Whether you would like to tutor the Australian youth in mathematics or simply expand your horizons in Sydney, Destinations Abroad will help place you where you will find the most adventageous for your future. These programs are available for one to four weeks and combine classroom study with in destination experiences that together will provide long lasting intuition and cultural awareness.

Participants are required to coordinate monthly meetings with their Destinations Abroad advisor to ensure all preparatory documentation is complete. Participants are also required to make full payment on their short-term programs prior to leaving for their study abroad or mission work programs.

Year-long programs in Australia

Although these programs are provided through a third-party partner, Destinations Abroad is available 24/7 to accommodate your every need. In addition, our third-party partners have dedicated local representatives to each our year-long participants to ensure our vision and mission statement are carried out. For details on our year-long programs please contact an Advisor at Info@destinationsabroad.com.

About Australia
